Descriptive Text
Mendeskripsikan orang, tempat, atau benda secara spesifik
Definisi

Descriptive text adalah teks yang bertujuan menggambarkan sebuah objek, orang, hewan, atau tempat tertentu dan spesifik secara detail, sehingga pembaca dapat membayangkannya dengan jelas.

🎯
Social Function
  • Mendeskripsikan subjek tertentu secara detail
  • Memberikan gambaran visual kepada pembaca
  • Membuat pembaca seolah melihat langsung objek tersebut
🏗️
Generic Structure
  • Identification — Memperkenalkan subjek yang akan dideskripsikan
  • Description — Menjelaskan ciri-ciri, sifat, dan karakteristik subjek secara rinci
🔤
Language Features
  • Specific nouns (kata benda spesifik: my cat, the Eiffel Tower)
  • Simple present tense (untuk fakta yang masih berlaku)
  • Detailed adjectives (beautiful, enormous, sparkling)
  • Relating verbs: is, has, looks, seems, appears
  • Figurative language (simile, metaphor)
⚠️
Perbedaan kunci: Descriptive text mendeskripsikan subjek SPESIFIK (contoh: "My dog, Max"), sedangkan Report text mendeskripsikan kategori/kelas UMUM (contoh: "Dogs in general").
📝 Contoh — Descriptive Text
[Identification]
My house is a cozy two-story building located on a quiet street in Bandung. It is my favourite place in the world.

[Description]
The exterior is painted light yellow, giving it a warm and welcoming appearance. The front yard is filled with various colourful flowers, especially roses and bougainvillea. Inside, the living room has high ceilings and large windows that let in plenty of natural light. The walls are decorated with family photographs and traditional Javanese batik paintings.
2
Recount Text
Menceritakan kembali peristiwa masa lalu secara kronologis
Definisi

Recount text adalah teks yang menceritakan kembali pengalaman atau peristiwa yang telah terjadi di masa lalu secara kronologis (berurutan) untuk tujuan menghibur atau memberi informasi kepada pembaca.

🎯
Social Function
  • Menceritakan kembali peristiwa masa lalu
  • Menghibur atau memberikan informasi
  • Berbagi pengalaman pribadi
🏗️
Generic Structure
  • Orientation — Who, when, where (pengenalan)
  • Events — Serangkaian peristiwa secara berurutan
  • Re-orientation — Komentar atau kesimpulan (opsional)
🔤
Language Features
  • Simple past tense dominan
  • Time connectives: first, then, after that, next, finally
  • Action verbs: went, saw, ate, played
  • Specific participants (nama orang, tempat)
  • Chronological order (urutan waktu)
📝 Contoh — Recount Text
[Orientation]
Last Saturday, my family and I visited Taman Safari in Bogor. The weather was sunny and perfect for an outdoor trip.

[Events]
First, we bought our tickets at the entrance gate and entered the main zone by car. We saw lions, zebras, and giraffes grazing calmly along the road. Then, we stopped at the feeding area where we could hand-feed the deer. My little sister was so excited. After that, we watched an animal show featuring trained orangutans and dolphins.

[Re-orientation]
It was truly a memorable experience for our family. I am looking forward to visiting again someday.
3
Report Text
Menyajikan informasi faktual tentang suatu kategori/kelas
Definisi

Report text adalah teks faktual yang mendeskripsikan sesuatu secara umum (kelas/kategori), bukan individu tertentu. Teks ini menyajikan informasi objektif dan ilmiah tentang fenomena alam, hewan, benda, atau konsep.

🎯
Social Function
  • Menyajikan informasi faktual dan ilmiah
  • Mendeskripsikan kategori/kelas benda atau makhluk
  • Memberikan pengetahuan umum kepada pembaca
🏗️
Generic Structure
  • General Classification — Memperkenalkan topik secara umum
  • Description — Menjelaskan fakta: ciri fisik, habitat, perilaku, fungsi, dll.
🔤
Language Features
  • Generic nouns (dolphins, volcanoes — bukan "my dolphin")
  • Simple present tense untuk fakta umum
  • Technical vocabulary (istilah ilmiah)
  • Passive voice dan relating verbs
  • Bahasa objektif, impersonal
📝 Contoh — Report Text
[General Classification]
Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals belonging to the family Delphinidae. They are found in oceans and seas around the world.

[Description]
Dolphins typically have streamlined bodies, which allow them to swim at speeds of up to 60 km/h. They use a system called echolocation to navigate and find prey. Dolphins are social animals that live in groups called pods, which can consist of up to hundreds of individuals. They communicate through clicks, whistles, and other vocalizations. Dolphins are carnivores and primarily feed on fish and squid.

Narrative Text
Menceritakan kisah dengan konflik dan penyelesaian
Definisi

Narrative text adalah teks yang menceritakan sebuah kisah — biasanya fiktif, legendaris, atau imajinatif — dengan tujuan menghibur pembaca sekaligus menyampaikan nilai moral atau pesan kehidupan.

🏗️
Generic Structure
  • Orientation — Perkenalan tokoh, latar, dan waktu
  • Complication — Konflik/masalah yang muncul
  • Resolution — Penyelesaian konflik
  • Re-orientation / Coda — Pesan moral atau penutup (opsional)
🔤
Language Features
  • Simple past tense dominan
  • Action verbs dan descriptive language
  • Time connectives: once upon a time, suddenly, meanwhile
  • Dialogue antar karakter
  • Specific characters (protagonist & antagonist)
📚
Jenis Narrative Text
  • Fairy tale (dongeng: Cinderella)
  • Legend (legenda: Sangkuriang, Roro Jonggrang)
  • Fable (fabel: The Tortoise and the Hare)
  • Myth (mitos)
  • Short story / novel
📝 Contoh — Narrative Text (Singkat)
[Orientation] Once upon a time, in a small village near a deep forest, there lived a kind-hearted girl named Risa.

[Complication] One day, Risa's mother fell seriously ill. The only cure was a rare flower that grew deep inside the forest. The forest was said to be guarded by a dangerous dragon. Despite her fear, Risa decided to enter the forest alone.

[Resolution] As she ventured deeper, she found the dragon sleeping. Carefully, she picked the flower and ran back without waking it. Her mother recovered, and the village celebrated Risa's bravery.

[Coda] Courage and love can overcome even the greatest of fears.

Comparative & Superlative Degree
Tingkatan perbandingan dalam bahasa Inggris
Definisi

Degree of Comparison adalah tingkatan yang digunakan untuk membandingkan sifat atau kualitas antara dua atau lebih benda/orang. Terdapat tiga tingkatan: Positive, Comparative, dan Superlative.

DegreeFungsiStrukturContoh
PositivePerbandingan setara (sama)as + adj + asShe is as tall as her sister.
ComparativeMembandingkan 2 hal (lebih dari)adj-er + than / more + adj + thanThis box is heavier than that one.
SuperlativeMembandingkan 3+ hal (paling)the + adj-est / the most + adjShe is the smartest student here.

Passive Voice
Kalimat pasif dalam berbagai bentuk waktu
Definisi

Passive voice adalah struktur kalimat di mana objek dari kalimat aktif menjadi subjek dalam kalimat pasif. Fokus beralih dari pelaku aksi ke penerima aksi.

Rumus Umum
Active: Subject + Verb + Object
Passive: Subject (was object) + To Be + V3 (past participle) + [by + agent]
TenseActive FormulaPassive FormulaContoh Pasif
Present SimpleS + V1(s/es) + Ois/are + V3The letter is written by Ana.
Past SimpleS + V2 + Owas/were + V3The cake was eaten by Tom.
Present ContinuousS + is/are + V-ing + Ois/are + being + V3The road is being repaired.
Past ContinuousS + was/were + V-ing + Owas/were + being + V3The house was being built.
Present PerfectS + have/has + V3 + Ohave/has + been + V3The work has been done.
Past PerfectS + had + V3 + Ohad + been + V3The car had been fixed.
Future SimpleS + will + V1 + Owill + be + V3The report will be submitted.
Modal PassiveS + modal + V1 + Omodal + be + V3The rules must be followed.
💡
Kapan menggunakan Passive? (1) Pelaku tidak diketahui: "My phone was stolen." (2) Pelaku tidak penting: "English is spoken worldwide." (3) Fokus pada penerima aksi: "The prize was awarded to her."
📝 Contoh Konversi Active → Passive
ActiveThe students clean the classroom every day.
PassiveThe classroom is cleaned by the students every day.

ActiveThey were building a new bridge last year.
PassiveA new bridge was being built last year.

Preference
Mengungkapkan kesukaan atau pilihan
Definisi

Preference adalah ungkapan untuk menyatakan pilihan atau kesukaan terhadap satu hal dibanding hal lain. Dalam bahasa Inggris terdapat beberapa pola berbeda.

1
prefer + noun/gerund + TO + noun/gerund
Untuk preferensi umum/kebiasaan. Contoh: "I prefer swimming to jogging." — Gunakan TO, BUKAN "than".
2
prefer + to-infinitive + rather than + gerund/infinitive
Contoh: "I prefer to stay home rather than go to the party."
3
would rather + base verb + than + base verb
Untuk preferensi spesifik/situasional saat ini. Contoh: "I'd rather watch a movie than study tonight." — Diikuti bare infinitive (tanpa to).
4
would sooner + base verb + than + base verb
Lebih formal, sama artinya dengan "would rather". Contoh: "They would sooner fly than drive."
⚠️
Kesalahan Umum: "I prefer reading than watching TV" — SALAH! Gunakan TO: "I prefer reading to watching TV." | "I'd rather to stay" — SALAH! Gunakan bare infinitive: "I'd rather stay."

Direct & Indirect Speech
Kalimat langsung dan tidak langsung
💬
Direct Speech
  • Mengutip ucapan secara PERSIS menggunakan tanda kutip
  • Struktur: Reporting clause + "quoted words"
  • Tidak ada perubahan tense, pronoun, atau time adverb
  • Contoh: She said, "I am very tired."
📣
Indirect Speech
  • Melaporkan ucapan TANPA mengutip langsung
  • Tanda kutip dihilangkan
  • Terjadi perubahan: tense, pronoun, time/place adverb
  • Contoh: She said that she was very tired.

Past Perfect Tense
Tindakan yang selesai sebelum peristiwa lain di masa lalu
Definisi

Past Perfect digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu tindakan yang telah selesai terjadi lebih dahulu sebelum tindakan lain di masa lampau. Menunjukkan urutan kronologis dua peristiwa masa lalu.

Rumus Past Perfect
Subject + had + V3 (past participle) + [complement]
Berlaku untuk SEMUA subjek (I/you/he/she/it/we/they)
📅 Urutan Kejadian
💡
Past Perfect (had + V3) = tindakan yang terjadi LEBIH DAHULU | Simple Past (V2) = tindakan yang terjadi BELAKANGAN
Signal WordsContoh Kalimat
by the timeBy the time I arrived, she had already left.
beforeShe had studied English before she moved abroad.
afterAfter he had finished dinner, he went to bed.
whenWhen we arrived, the movie had already started.
already / just / neverI had never seen the ocean before that day.
📝 Contoh Past Perfect
Benar: "I was not hungry at dinner because I had eaten a large lunch." (makan siang terjadi lebih dulu)

Benar: "By 2020, they had lived in that city for ten years."

Salah: "When I arrived, the movie already started." ← Harus: had already started

Adverb of Manner
Kata keterangan cara — menjawab pertanyaan "How?"
Definisi

Adverb of manner adalah kata keterangan yang menjelaskan bagaimana (how) suatu tindakan dilakukan. Adverb ini memodifikasi kata kerja (verb), adjective, atau adverb lain.

🔤
Pembentukan Regular (-ly)
  • careful → carefully
  • quick → quickly
  • beautiful → beautifully
  • slow → slowly
  • happy → happily (y→i+ly)
⚡
Bentuk Tidak Beraturan (Irregular)
  • good → well (bukan "goodly")
  • fast → fast (bukan "fastly")
  • hard → hard (bukan "hardly"*)
  • late → late (bukan "lately"*)
  • high → high (bukan "highly"*)
📍
Posisi dalam Kalimat
  • Setelah verb intransitive: "She sings beautifully."
  • Setelah object: "He read the book quickly."
  • Sebelum adjective/adverb lain: "She is incredibly talented."
  • Di awal kalimat (penekanan): "Suddenly, he left."
⚠️
Perhatian: "Hardly" bukan bentuk adverb dari "hard" — artinya berbeda! "hardly" = hampir tidak (scarcely). "hard" = dengan giat/keras. Contoh: "He works hard" (giat) vs. "He hardly works" (hampir tidak bekerja).

goodwellHe plays the guitar well.
fastfastShe runs fast every morning.

Agreement & Disagreement
Mengungkapkan persetujuan dan ketidaksetujuan
Definisi

Ungkapan agreement (persetujuan) dan disagreement (ketidaksetujuan) digunakan untuk merespons pendapat atau pernyataan orang lain dalam percakapan.

✅
Strong Agreement (Setuju Kuat)
  • "I completely agree with you."
  • "Absolutely! You're right."
  • "I couldn't agree more!"
  • "You can say that again!"
  • "Exactly! That's precisely my point."
🤝
Mild Agreement (Setuju Biasa)
  • "I think you're right."
  • "That's a fair point."
  • "I suppose you're right."
  • "That makes sense."
  • "I agree to some extent."
🤔
Polite Disagreement (Tidak Setuju Halus)
  • "I see your point, but I think differently."
  • "I'm not sure I agree entirely."
  • "I'm afraid I have a different view."
  • "That's interesting, however..."
  • "I understand, but I'm not convinced."
❌
Strong Disagreement (Tidak Setuju Kuat)
  • "I totally disagree with that."
  • "With all due respect, I think you're mistaken."
  • "I strongly disagree."
  • "That's not correct at all."
  • "I'm afraid that's completely wrong."
💡
"I couldn't agree more" — meskipun ada kata "couldn't" (negatif), ungkapan ini adalah ekspresi SETUJU SANGAT KUAT. Artinya: "Saya tidak bisa setuju lebih dari ini" = saya sangat setuju. Jangan tertukar!
